Who We Are & What We Do

Manala Foundation is a community-driven Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) rooted in Kagongwa, Kahama, Shinyanga, Tanzania. Our core mission is to empower individuals and communities by investing in three critical pillars of development: Education, Health, and Economic Empowerment.

Vision: To build an educated, healthy, and economically self-sufficient society in Tanzania.

Partner With Us

Our Core Pillars of Development

Education

Building infrastructure, providing school supplies, and offering scholarships to give children and youth the key to a better future.

Healthcare

Ensuring access to medical services, essential medicines, and critical health education in rural communities.

Economic Empowerment

Providing entrepreneurship training and microfinance support for women and youth groups to foster job creation and income generation.
